\begin{align*}
\detname{B} 
&= 8x -2x^2 - x^3
= -x(x^2 - 2x + 8)
= -x(x-2)(x-4)
\end{align*}
%
And thus, $\detname{B}=0$ when $x = 0$, $x = 2$, or $x = 4$.
